Marinated Mushroom Salad(Salade de Champignons)

    Chef's Note As much as possible, salad greens should be dressed à la minute.
      Geographic Region

        France

        Main Dish
      • 2 pounds Button mushrooms, cleaned and sliced thin.

      • 8 ounces Red bell pepper, washed, seeded, diced into ¼-inch (0.63-centimeter) pieces.

          Dressing

        1. 2 ounces White vinegar

        2. 2 ounces White wine

        3. 2 ounces Lemon juice

        4. 1 ounce Sugar, granulated

        5. 1 ounce Basil, fresh, washed, chopped

        6. 1 ounce Oregano, fresh, washed, chopped

        7. ¼ teaspoon Marjoram, fresh Salt, Black pepper.

        8. 3 heads Romaine lettuce, washed, cut or shredded into fine strips.

        9. 8 ounces Green peas

          Serving Size

          3 ounces (85 grams)

          Yield

          10 servings
